Access Odoo Sign
Navigate to the Sign app from the Odoo home menu

How to get there

Option A — Home Menu

  • Click the grid icon (top-left) to open the Home Menu
  • Search for "Sign" or scroll to find the Sign app
  • Click on it to open

Option B — Direct URL

Bookmark: kebonyprod.odoo.com/odoo/sign

The Sign menu bar

Documents — Signature requests sent & received
Templates — Reusable document templates
Reports — Signing activity overview

What you'll see

Sign > Documents (empty state)
📄

The Documents view shows all signature requests.

Empty at first — your signed documents will appear here.

Two sub-menus under Documents:
• My Documents — requests you created
• All Documents — all requests across the team

Upload a PDF Template
Create a reusable template from any PDF document

Two ways to upload

Drag & Drop

Drag a PDF file from your computer directly into the Templates view. The upload overlay appears automatically.

Click "Upload PDF"

  • Go to Sign → Templates
  • Click the Upload PDF button (top-left)
  • Select your PDF file from the file picker
Tip: Templates are reusable! Upload a contract, NDA, or policy once, then send it to different recipients each time.

What happens after upload

Sign > Templates > Your Document.pdf

The Template Editor opens with your PDF displayed in the center.

On the left panel you'll see:

  • Documents — your uploaded PDF(s)
  • Signers — roles for signing
  • Field palette — drag-and-drop fields

At the top: Send Sign Now Share

Multi-document: Click "Add" next to Documents to bundle multiple PDFs into a single signature request.

How to place a field

  1. Select a Signer role (left panel, under "Signers")
  2. Drag a field type from the palette onto the PDF
  3. Position and resize the field on the document
  4. Repeat for each field you need

Signers (roles)

What are signers?

Each field belongs to a signer role. When you send the document, each role is assigned to a real person.

Example: "Signer 1" = the customer, "Signer 2" = the Kebony representative.

Add a second signer

  • Click Add next to "Signers"
  • A new signer role appears (e.g. "Signer 2")
  • Fields assigned to Signer 2 will show in a different color
Signing order: Signers sign in order. Signer 2 receives their request only after Signer 1 has completed signing.
Smart prefill: Name, Email, Company, and Address fields auto-populate from the recipient's Odoo contact record.

Send for Signature
Assign recipients to each role and send the request

Sending a signature request

Click "Send"

  1. Click the Send button (top-left of template editor)
  2. A dialog opens asking you to assign recipients
  3. For each signer role, type a name or email
  4. Odoo suggests existing contacts as you type
  5. Optionally add a subject and message
  6. Click "Send" to dispatch the request

Alternative: Sign Now

For self-signing

Click Sign Now if you are one of the signers. This opens the document for your immediate signature.

Alternative: Share

Share a link

Click Share to generate a signing link that you can copy and send via any channel (email, chat, etc.).

What the recipient sees

Email received by recipient

Subject: Signature Request — [Document Name]

Body: "You have been requested to sign the following document..."

Contains a Review & Sign button linking to a secure signing page.

The signing experience

Signing page (recipient's browser)

The recipient sees the PDF with highlighted fields to fill in:

  • Click on a Signature field → draw, type, or upload
  • Name/Email/Date fields auto-fill when possible
  • All required fields must be completed
  • Click Validate & Sign to finalize
No Odoo account needed! Recipients sign via a secure web link. They don't need to log in or create an account.

Track & Complete
Monitor signing progress and download completed documents

Document status tracking

Navigate to Documents

Go to Sign → Documents → All Documents

Each request shows its current status:

IN PROGRESS Waiting for one or more signers
SIGNED All parties have signed
REFUSED A signer declined to sign
CANCELED Request was canceled

Download the signed document

  • Click on a completed (Signed) document
  • The signed PDF is available for download
  • A Certificate of Completion is attached — this is your audit trail

Certificate of Completion

Audit trail document
The certificate includes:
  • Document ID — unique identifier
  • Signatory details — name, email, IP address
  • Timestamps — when each party signed
  • Cryptographic hash — proves document integrity

Legally binding under eIDAS (EU) and ESIGN Act (US).

Reminders & follow-up

  • Odoo can send automatic reminders to signers who haven't responded
  • You can also manually resend a request from the document view
  • Set validity periods on templates (e.g. expires in 14 days)
All parties receive a copy. Once fully signed, every signer automatically receives the final signed PDF by email.

Bonus: Sign from a Purchase Order or Sale Order

Request signatures directly from Odoo records

From a Purchase Order

  1. Open the PO in Purchase → Orders
  2. Click the ⚙ Actions (gear) menu
  3. Select "Request Signature"
  4. Choose a template or let Odoo generate one from the PO
  5. Assign the recipient (vendor/customer)
  6. Click Send

The signed document attaches to the PO record automatically.

From a Sale Order / Quotation

Option A: Portal signature (lightweight)

Customer signs on the Odoo portal page to confirm the order. No separate document needed.

Enabled in Sales → Settings → Online Signature

Option B: Full Sign request

Same as PO: ⚙ Actions → Request Signature on the SO record.

From any Odoo record

⚙ Actions menu > Request Signature

Available on any Odoo record

Invoices, CRM opportunities, HR contracts, custom records — the gear icon always offers "Request Signature" when Sign is installed.

Key benefit over DocuSign: The signed document automatically links back to the originating Odoo record (PO, SO, invoice). No manual filing needed.
Database is neutralized for testing — no emails will actually be sent from the test environment. You can practice freely.
