Architecture Principles

Four structural rules that shape the entire product model

1. No Dimensional Variants

Odoo 19 merged packaging and UoM. A product sold as "Pack of 162 × 3.2 lm" needs packaging-level UoMs that depend on length. Each dimensional product = its own product.template.

2. Metric / Imperial Separation

Metric and imperial are separate templates linked by a shared Master Item Code. Metric is the engineering source of truth; imperial derives from it.

Concept Metric Imperial
Name 1127-M-3.2 1127-I-10.5
UoM m (meter) LF (linear foot)

3. Treatment ≠ Variant

Treatment (fire retardant, colour, brushing) is tracked at lot level only. Product identity remains purely dimensional.

Without this rule: ~70 cross-sections × ~20 lengths × 4 treatments × 2 systems = ~11,200 SKUs. Current catalogue: ~490.

4. Three-Level Hierarchy

Master Item (e.g. 1127) └── Master Variant (e.g. 1127-3.2) ├── Metric Product (1127-M-3.2) └── Imperial Product (1127-I-10.5)

Measurement Doctrine

Three dimensions of quantity & the critical volume distinction
Three Dimensions of Quantity

Linear Operational

meters (m), lm, LF — normalised to meters internally

Board Count Informational

Integer boards — ROUND_UP at threshold 0.95

Volume Economic Truth

m³ — all KPIs, pricing, margin analysis in m³

Conversion Constants
FT_PER_M = 3.280839895 M_PER_FT = 1 / FT_PER_M FT3_PER_M3 = 35.3146667
Critical — Two Volume Fields
Field Technical Name Unit Usage
Normalised x_studio_volume_m3 Always m³/lm Canonical All economic calcs
Localised volume (Odoo) m³/lm or ft³/lf Transport only Never for metrics
Formula: Total volume = linear_meters × x_studio_volume_m3
Never use product.volume for economic calculations.
Key Formulas
Derived Field Formula
Volume (m³/lm) thickness(m) × width(m)
lm per m³ 1 / x_studio_volume_m3
lm per m² 1 / width(m)
Weight per lm density × volume(m³/lm)
Boards ROUND(linear_m / length_m), threshold 0.95
Total volume linear_m × x_studio_volume_m3

Supply Chain Rules

Four replenishment strategies — per-product assignment based on market maturity

Make to Order

Buffer: None

Trigger: Min. process batch reached

Lead time: Procurement + production + transport

New / low-volume products

ATO — White Wood

Buffer: White wood (untreated)

Trigger: Min. white wood stock

Lead time: Production + transport

Start from autoclave stage

ATO — Brown Wood

Buffer: Brown wood (treated RS)

Trigger: Min. brown wood stock

Lead time: Machining + transport

Radiata — machining only

Make to Stock

Buffer: Finished goods

Trigger: Min. FG stock breach

Lead time: Transport only

High-volume / mature

MTO: [Procurement] → [Stacking] → [Autoclave] → [Dryer] → [Machining] → [Transport] ← full lead time ATO-WW: [White Wood Buffer] → [Autoclave] → [Dryer] → [Machining] → [Transport] ATO-BW: [Brown Wood Buffer] → [Machining] → [Transport] MTS: [FG Buffer] → [Transport]
Products can transition: MTO (introduction) → ATO → MTS (mature) as demand stabilises. Per-product assignment awaiting COO input.

Metrics Computation Architecture

The mixin, recompute hierarchy, and wood-only rule
The Mixin: kebony.metrics.mixin

Abstract model inherited by: stock.quant, stock.lot, stock.move, sale.order.line, account.move.line, product.template.

Method Purpose
_kebony_linear_qty_in_m() Normalise any quantity to meters
_kebony_boards_from_linear() Board count (ROUND_UP 0.95)
_kebony_volume_m3() Volume via x_studio_volume_m3

Traceability & FIFO Integrity

Lot-level identity, treatment separation, and per-lot cost tracking

Lot-Level Identity

Each physical production batch receives a unique stock.lot record carrying:

  • Treatment info (fire retardant, colour, brushing)
  • Metrics: linear meters, boards, volume
  • Pack reservation: link to reserved SO
  • Legacy fields: x_studio_boards, x_studio_linear_feet

Product ≠ Treatment

Treatment is a post-production attribute applied during or after manufacturing.

It is never part of the product identity or SKU. This prevents combinatorial SKU explosion.

Product identity = dimensional specification only.

FIFO Integrity

Odoo 19's lot-valuated FIFO ensures per-lot cost tracking:

  • lot_valuated = True
    → per-lot cost tracking
  • tracking = 'lot'
    → mandatory lot assignment
  • categ_id
    → GL account routing for valuation

Governance Framework

Master Data Guardian, RACI, change control & critical rules
Master Data Guardian

A Super Master Data Guardian must be nominated — single point of accountability for product data integrity across the organisation.

  • Authority: No product CRUD without Guardian validation
  • Scope: All entities, all product types, all governed fields
  • Cadence: Weekly review, monthly audit
Change Control Process
1. REQUEST → Fields, current → proposed, justification 2. REVIEW → Cross-domain & financial impact 3. APPROVE → Guardian sign-off (or escalate) 4. EXECUTE → Applied in Odoo 5. VALIDATE → Metrics, orders, planning intact? 6. DOCUMENT → Change logged in register
RACI Matrix
Field Family R A C I
Identity MDG COO Sales All
Geometry — Metric CTO MDG Prod. Finance
Geometry — Imperial CTO MDG US Sales Finance
Packaging Planning MDG Logistics Sales

Manufacturing Planning MDG Eng. Finance

Critical Rules
  • No bulk updates without Guardian approval
  • No Studio field creation without documentation
  • No field renaming (Studio limitation)
  • Metric fields read-only for non-Engineering
  • Lifecycle transitions are one-way
  • Imperial derives from Metric

Strategic Impact

Why Product Master Data is infrastructure — not just a catalogue

1. Cubic-Based Margin Logic

Every financial KPI reconciles to m³ through the normalised volume ratio

2. Dryer-Centric Manufacturing

Planning family links products to process parameters and bottleneck constraints

3. FIFO Integrity

Lot-valuated tracking with mandatory lot assignment prevents cost mixing

4. Multi-Entity Operations

Shared product masters across BNV (manufacturing), INC (distribution), Holding

5. Analytical Accounting

Product category and classification drive GL routing and analytic distribution

6. Trade Compliance

HS codes, commodity codes, and country of origin are first-class governed fields

7. No SKU Explosion

Treatment at lot level, no dimensional variants, three-level hierarchy — ~490 products instead of ~11,200

Design principle: Product Master Data is infrastructure. It must be treated with the same rigour as financial chart of accounts or manufacturing Bill of Materials.
