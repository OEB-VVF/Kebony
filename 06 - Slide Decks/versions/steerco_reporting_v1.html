Data Stewardship — One Owner per KPI Family

No KPI without an owner. No report without a source definition.
KPI Family Steward Source System
Revenue & Invoicing Controller Odoo Accounting
Sales Pipeline Sales Director Odoo Sales
Cash & Receivables Controller Odoo Accounting
COGS & Margin Controller Odoo (custom module)
Inventory & Stock Warehouse Manager Odoo Inventory
Production Output Production Manager Odoo MRP
Purchasing & Spend Purchasing Manager Odoo Purchase
Quality & Scrap Quality Manager Odoo Quality

Steward Responsibilities

  • Define: What exactly does this KPI measure?
  • Source: Which Odoo field / report is the single source?
  • Quality: Is the data complete, timely, accurate?
  • Cutoff: When is the month closed? No late changes.
  • Escalate: Flag data issues before they reach the board
One version of the truth: If the Sales Director reports revenue by area, and the Controller reports revenue by area, they MUST match — because both pull from the same Odoo invoice pivot. Disagreements = data quality issue, not a reporting issue.
No more Excel kingdoms. Every operational KPI lives in Odoo. Personal spreadsheets are analysis tools, not source-of-truth systems.
