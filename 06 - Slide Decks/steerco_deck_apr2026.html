INC (US) — Customisation support Healthy

88 tickets raised, of which 11 still open in the period — open ratio 12.5%. Operational rhythm is stable.

Typology — rule-of-thumb split into three buckets:

Bugs ≈ ⅓
Features ≈ ⅓
How-to ≈ ⅓
Bugs — resolved D+1  ·  New functionality  ·  How-to / enablement
Read-across: the "new functionality" third = customer-pull roadmap pipeline (not noise). The "how-to" third = training / documentation gap — cheap to reduce with enablement material.

Why this matters for SteerCo

  • Ticketing = ground-up signal — captures issue dynamics and emerging requests directly from the field, independent of top-down roadmap.
  • Customer-pull pipeline: ~30% of tickets are feature requests. These are not support load — they are the next cycle of product scope feeding itself from usage.
  • Enablement lever: 30% how-to volume points to where a small training investment would disproportionately reduce inbound noise.
  • Bug health visible in real time: next-day closure of ~30% bug tickets means no hidden backlog accumulating.
Discipline ask: Belgium team to start using the ticketing system consistently so we have the same ground-up visibility on the BE side. Not yet live as a data source.
