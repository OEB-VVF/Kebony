Marketing Tools Benchmark

Odoo 19 Marketing Suite vs. Specialist Software — Honest Assessment
Prepared for Kebony CMO — March 2026 | Confidential

Executive Summary

Odoo 19 includes five marketing modules (Email, Automation, SMS, Social, Events) bundled at no extra cost with the ERP. For a B2B manufacturer/distributor like Kebony, they cover 70–80% of typical marketing needs. The remaining 20–30% — advanced automation workflows, email deliverability, social listening, and multi-touch attribution — require specialist tools if the marketing team's ambitions go beyond newsletters and event management.

Bottom line: Start with Odoo. Add specialists only where you hit a concrete wall — not on speculation.

📧 Email Marketing

What Odoo 19 Can Do

  • Drag-and-drop email builder with mobile-responsive templates
  • AI-powered subject line and content suggestions (new in v19)
  • A/B testing on subject lines and content
  • Advanced segmentation based on behaviour, demographics, purchase history
  • Real-time stats: open rate, bounce rate, CTR, revenue tracking
  • Link Tracker with UTM parameters
  • GDPR-compliant unsubscribe and opt-in management
  • Native CRM/Sales integration — leads, orders, revenue per campaign tracked automatically

💬 SMS Marketing

What Odoo 19 Can Do

  • Campaign creation with dynamic placeholders
  • A/B testing for SMS campaigns
  • Scheduling, opt-out compliance, blacklist management
  • Link tracking with revenue attribution
  • Credit-based pricing (~$0.033/SMS to US)
  • Integrated with email and social campaigns

What Odoo Lacks

  • No two-way SMS conversations (reply handling)
  • No keyword-based auto-responses
  • No MMS support (images/media)
  • No BYO-carrier option (locked to Odoo gateway)
  • Per-credit pricing can be expensive at scale vs. volume contracts
Verdict: Adequate for B2B — SMS is rarely a primary channel for B2B timber distribution. Odoo's SMS module covers what Kebony would need (delivery notifications, event reminders, urgent customer alerts). Two-way SMS and MMS are nice-to-haves, not must-haves in this context.

🎪 Events

What Odoo 19 Can Do

  • Full event lifecycle: creation, ticketing, registration, promotion, follow-up
  • Multi-slot scheduling (attendees pick specific sessions)
  • Schedule & Tracks management (talks, demos, workshops)
  • Live broadcast via YouTube integration
  • Online exhibitors and sponsor showcase
  • Virtual conferencing via Jitsi integration
  • Booth management with tiered pricing
  • Barcode/QR code attendance scanning
  • Event gamification with quizzes
  • Native CRM integration (leads from events)
  • Automated triggers: before/after event start (new in v19)

What Specialists Offer Extra

  • Cvent ($15–50K/yr): RFID/NFC badge tracking, venue sourcing, lead retrieval for exhibitors, branded mobile app
  • Eventbrite (pay-per-ticket): Massive public discovery marketplace (millions of visitors)
  • Bizzabo: AI-powered attendee networking/matching, hybrid event platform
Verdict: Genuinely Strong — Odoo Events is the best module in the marketing suite. For Kebony's needs (distributor days, trade shows, training events, product launches), it's fully capable. The native CRM integration means event leads flow directly into the sales pipeline. No specialist needed unless running 1000+ person conferences.

🎯 Recommendation for Kebony

Phase 1 — Start with Odoo (Now)

  • Email Marketing — distributor newsletters, product launches, customer communications
  • Events — distributor days, trade shows, training sessions
  • Social Media — LinkedIn scheduling, project showcases, product content
  • Marketing Automation — welcome sequences, event follow-ups, simple lead nurture
  • Benefit: zero additional cost, single source of truth with CRM/Sales/Inventory

Phase 2 — Add Supplements If Needed (3–6 months)

  • If email deliverability is a problem → evaluate ActiveCampaign (~€5K/yr) or Mailchimp (~€2K/yr) for critical campaigns only
  • If social needs grow → add Buffer (~€1.2K/yr) for optimal posting times and content calendar
  • If marketing team demands complex automation → evaluate HubSpot Marketing Hub, but only with a clear ROI case (it's €35K+/yr)

What NOT to Do

  • Don't buy HubSpot "just in case" — it's expensive and creates a data silo
  • Don't maintain two CRMs (Odoo + HubSpot) — that's a recipe for data chaos
  • Don't underestimate Odoo's native integration advantage — leads, orders, inventory, and marketing in one system is genuinely powerful for a manufacturer
